### Accommodation Recommendations

The FAME Doctoral School will take place at the Ada Byron Building, located at the University of Málaga (UMA) – Teatinos Campus Extension. The venue is directly connected to the city through Metro Line 1 (Universidad direction), making it easy to stay outside the campus area while enjoying Málaga.

For participants visiting Málaga, we recommend staying either:

* in the historic city centre, to enjoy the cultural and social life of the city, or
* near María Zambrano (Vialia) train station, which offers excellent public transport connections (metro, airport train, and high-speed rail).

From both areas, the trip to the venue typically takes 20–30 minutes by metro.

Please note that the prices below are indicative estimates for the school dates (September 30 – October 2, 2026) and may vary depending on booking date and availability. Note that MODELS'26 takes place concurrently in the same city, so demand for accommodation is expected to be high — we strongly recommend booking well in advance.

### Budget Hotels

1. [Carlos V Málaga](https://www.booking.com/hotel/es/carlos-v-malaga.html) (historic centre)
A simple and well-located hotel in Málaga’s historic centre, within walking distance of the main attractions and with convenient access to metro connections towards the university campus. A good option for participants who prefer a private room while staying in the city centre.
Approximate price: €95–135 per night.

2. [Hostal Victoria Málaga](https://www.hostalvictoriamalaga.com) (historic centre)
A simple and centrally located guesthouse for participants looking for private accommodation at a moderate cost while remaining close to metro connections. A solid budget pick.
Approximate price: €75–110 per night.

3. [Hotel Solymar](https://www.booking.com/hotel/es/solymar-malaga.html) (Huelin / near María Zambrano station)
A budget-friendly hotel located close to the beach and within walking distance of Málaga train and metro connections. It offers a convenient balance between cost and transport accessibility.
Approximate price: €70–120 per night.

4. [The Lights City Rooms](https://www.booking.com/hotel/es/the-lights-city-rooms-malaga.html) (historic centre)
A stylish and centrally located property offering modern, well-equipped rooms in the heart of Málaga. Close to Calle Larios and the main metro connections.
Approximate price: €80–130 per night.

### Apartments and Short-Term Rentals

Participants may also find apartments and studio flats at competitive prices through platforms such as [Airbnb](https://www.airbnb.com) and [Booking.com](https://www.booking.com). These can be a cost-effective option, especially for participants staying for the full MODELS'26 conference week, and may offer kitchen facilities and more space than a standard hotel room. We recommend searching within the historic centre or near the María Zambrano station for the best transport connections to the venue.

Participants are encouraged to book early, as accommodation in Málaga fills up quickly during the MODELS conference week.

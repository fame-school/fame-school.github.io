## Reaching Ada Byron Building

### By Metro (recommended)

To reach the Ada Byron Building in Málaga by metro, take Metro Line 1 (L1) towards Andalucía Tech and get off at Andalucía Tech station. From there, it is a short walk (5min) to the Ada Byron Building, located within the University of Málaga (UMA) ampliacion campus.

### By Bus

From the city centre, take Bus Line 11 to the final stop at University (Louis Pasteur). Then, continue either by metro to Andalucía Tech (Universidad → Andalucía Tech) or by Bus Line L to the Faculty of Computer Science, getting off at the final stop opposite the Ada Byron Building.

### By Car

There are plenty of parking spaces available on the university campus if you are arriving by car.
